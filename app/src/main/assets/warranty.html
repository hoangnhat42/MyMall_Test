Warranty Policy



MOBILE SOLUTION offers 3-month warranty on replaced screen under these conditions:

  1. MOBILE SOLUTION has the right to refuse service on any claims solely at its own discretion.

  2. The warranty includes factory screen defects, such as unresponsive or erratic touch, and dead pixels.

  3. The 6-month warranty is applicable only on the mobile screen replacement featuring the same benchmarks and capabilities of the original faulty screen.

  4. The warranty does not cover any other parts of the mobile.

  5. The warranty only covers manufacture defects, and does not encompass damage due to manual intervention.


The 3-month warranty is NOT valid under these conditions:

  1. If you don't hand over the non-working mobile part to the MOBILE SOLUTION technician, warranty will be valid only for 1 months.

  2. In case other components of the mobile are damaged, and the phone refuses to turn on and perform all the tasks it is supposed to do.

  3. If the phone is left partially working, following an unsuccessful repair attempt at home or by any other vendor.

  4. In case a phone model is known to have a particular manufacturing/performance issue that impairs the functioning of the screen.

  5. In case the mobile’s frame or body is damaged.

  6. In case the mobile is jail-broken.

  7. If the internal hardware is tampered with, especially through a self-repair attempt.

  8. The warranty will not cover phones that have been damaged negligently and intentionally.

  9. It is also not applicable to devices which have been repaired or tampered-with before, by anyone else other than MOBILE SOLUTION.

  10. In case the device is transferred or sold to another user. The second-hand user will not be entitled to the warranty.


To claim the 3-month warranty, all you have to do is:

Ask the repair expert fixing your broken phone screen to avail the warranty. After providing the necessary details, you’ll receive the warranty if all the prerequisites are fulfilled.


Once you have claimed the warranty, the MOBILE SOLUTION team will take about 48-72 hours to resolve your issue. However, it may take more, depending on the nature of your case.
